Fern Acres, HI Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Fern Acres is located on the eastern side of the island of Hawaii at 19°30′43″N 155°5′24″W (19.511846, -155.089878). It is bordered to the east by Hawaiian Acres, to the north by Mountain View, and to the west by Eden Roc. To the south is the Puna Forest Reserve. The community is 17 miles (27 km) south of Hilo and 16 miles (26 km) east of the entrance to Hawaii Volcanoes National Park.
