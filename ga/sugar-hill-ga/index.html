Sugar Hill, GA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Sugar Hill is a city in northern Gwinnett County in the U.S. state of Georgia, included within the Atlanta-Sandy Springs-Marietta Metropolitan Statistical Area. The population was 18,522 as of the 2010 census, making it the fourth-largest city in Gwinnett County. As of 2020, the estimated population was 23,994.
